Chart info

How the BTC, MSTR, and portfolio models are calculated.

BTC power law

The BTC tab models price as price = A × days^n, where days are counted from Bitcoin’s Jan. 3, 2009 genesis date. The default preset is Giovanni/Santostasi style: 10^-17 × days^5.82.

Rainbow bands

The colored bands are multipliers around the base BTC power-law line. They are valuation zones, not separate formulas.

ATH / ATL timing projection

The overlay starts from an anchor ATL date, projects 1,064 days to ATH, then 364 days back to ATL. The recovery point is 700 days after ATL and is used by the Strategy model.

Portfolio projection

The Portfolio tab combines user-entered BTC holdings and MSTR shares into a single projected portfolio value. Optional BTC and MSTR DCA settings add new purchases on a daily, weekly, or monthly cadence using the price projected for each purchase date.

MSTR projection

The MSTR tab uses the same BTC cycle dates and BTC prices to project mNAV, sats per share, and MSTR price. MSTR price is calculated as BTC × sats/share ÷ 100,000,000 × mNAV. Its optional rainbow can use either editable mNAV bands or BTC-derived power-law bands converted through projected sats/share and projected mNAV.

The sats/share calculation follows your spreadsheet logic: average effective mNAV creates a BTC-per-share yield, then that yield fades as implied BTC holdings approach the practical max BTC holdings.
